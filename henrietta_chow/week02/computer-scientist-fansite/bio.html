Who is Yukihiro Matsumoto?

Early Life

Completely self-taught on computer programming, Yukihiro Matsumoto is a Japanese computer scientist and software programmer.

Personal Life

Matz is married and has four children. He is a member of the Mormon Church and has previously served as a missionary and is now a counsellor at his church.

Work

Matz is the creator of the Ruby and MRuby Programming Language. He is currently the Chief Architect of Ruby at Heroku, an online cloud platform-as-a-service.

Matz says...

"We need to focus on humans, on how humans care about doing programming or operating the application of the machines. We are the masters. They are the slaves."
