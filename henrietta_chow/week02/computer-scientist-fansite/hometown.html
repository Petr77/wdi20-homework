Born on April 14, 1965 in the Osaka Prefecture of Japan, Yukihiro Matsumoto ihe was raised in Tottori Prefecture from the age of four.

About Osaka

Osaka is the second largest metropolitan area in Japan. It's also the second largest city in Japan (after Tokyo) and serves as an economic hub for the country.

Fun Facts

Historically a merchant city, Osaka has also been known as the "nation's kitchen" (天下の台所) and served as a center for the rice trade during the Edo period.
