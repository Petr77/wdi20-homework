Interesting Facts About Yukihiro Matsumoto

Fact #1

Matsumoto, nicknamed Matz, is known as one of the open source evangelists in Japan.

Fact #2

Ruby, released back in December 1995, is his first piece of software that has become known outside Japan.

Fun Fact #3

Matz was a C++ programmer before started designing Ruby. He programmed in C++ exclusively for two or three years.

"Smart people underestimate the ordinarity of ordinary people."
